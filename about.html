About Pride Crochet

Established in January 2022, Pride Crochet started off in a little one bedroom apartment in Peru, NE. Now it has grown into its new home at 600 Hoyt Street Peru,NE.

At Pride Crochet we want to help you find the most ideal product for you. Our team will work with you to select a design, color and product.

Many of our products are created at request of our customers. After filling out a request form our team reviews the form and determining the best way for the product to be produced and in your hands in the littlest time possible.

Check out this video and follow us for more videos on products and techniques that we use.

We also provide crocheting lessons and have a club that meets every other Tuesday. If you are interested in becoming a member or just want to learn more about crocheting please contact us.
